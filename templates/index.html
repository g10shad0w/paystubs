Payroll Tools

Upload a file, press the button, get your file back.

1  Paystub → Time Cards

Upload a W2 paystubs PDF. Get an Excel workbook with one formatted time card per employee, hours totaling each paystub.

Optional: days off per employee (JSON)

2  Time Card → PDF

Upload a timecard .xlsx workbook. Get a PDF with one employee per page. The Staff Schedule summary sheet is dropped automatically.

3  Combine Paystubs + Time Cards

Upload a paystubs PDF and a time cards PDF. Get one PDF with each employee's paystub on top and their matching time card below, one per page. Employees are matched automatically by name and hours.

Runs entirely on this computer. Time cards are a model total, not a real attendance record.
